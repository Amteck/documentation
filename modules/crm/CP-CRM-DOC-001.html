This How To guide provides instructions for COINS system administrators on the steps required to enter a new opportunity from within COINS. Always test this process in an up to date, non-live environment before attempting it on a LIVE environment. When performing this process on a LIVE environment we recommend that you have a working, complete backup of the LIVE environment.

Table of Contents

  3. Rainmaker Report

Entering a New Lead

  1. Navigate to the Amteck CRM Desktop > Click on the Opportunities Red Tile


  2. Click the Add Button


  3. Enter the appropriate information regarding the Opportunity on the Main Tab


  4. Enter the appropriate Location and Date on the Location / Date / Forecast Tab

  5. Click Save

Updating an Existing Opportunity

    Navigate to the Amteck CRM Desktop > Click on the Opportunities Red Tile

  1. Ensure your filters are set to All Active Opportunities


  2. Click the Hyperlink on the Opportunity you would like to update


  3. Click the Hyperlink on the Opportunity you would like to update
  4. Click on the Activities Tab and Enter any Activity related to Lead

  5. Click on the Notes Tab to enter any notes related to this Lead. Same as Award Comments in SharePoint
